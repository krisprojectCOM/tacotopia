Meet Kenny

A world renowned taco chef and the owner of Tacotopia.

His interest in Taco making started at childhood and eventually lead to him opening a small food truck in Santa Barbara California. Since then, he has opened several Tacotopias across the country, with crowds willing stand in line for hours, just to get a taste of his mouth-watering Tacos. Tacotopia has been celebrated by everyone from Leonardo DiCaprio to the NELK squad.

When asked about his passions in life he told us "I like to make tacos and provide customers with a unique dining experince"
